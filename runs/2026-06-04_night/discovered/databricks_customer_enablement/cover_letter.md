That belief is exactly why the <b>Customer Enablement Specialist</b> role at Databricks excites me. I'd love to bring my strength in <b>learning program design</b>, <b>adoption tracking</b>, and <b>stakeholder enablement</b> to a team helping new users discover what's possible.</p>

I build the guides and training plans that help 70+ cross-functional staff actually adopt new programs, and I lean on feedback analysis to refine delivery so the next cohort learns faster. Connecting learners to the right next step in their journey is the work I find most rewarding.</p>

I led a manager-effectiveness initiative that turned <b>2,000+ employee experiences</b> into actionable reports, identifying the real drivers behind engagement and guiding C-suite decisions. I'm comfortable tracking the metrics that matter—completion, growth, conversion—and reporting them clearly so partners can act, rather than letting data sit in a spreadsheet.</p>
