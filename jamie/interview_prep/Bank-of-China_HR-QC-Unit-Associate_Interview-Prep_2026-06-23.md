## B. BANKING RISK & COMPLIANCE (why this role exists)
- **Three Lines of Defense** — **1st** = business unit owns its controls (**HR = 1st line; this unit proves it**) · **2nd** = Risk/Compliance sets standards & monitors · **3rd** = Internal Audit, independent assurance. ← *memorize.*
- **OCC** — charters/examines national banks + federal branches of foreign banks (BOC NY's regulator).
- **Fed** — supervises bank holding cos + foreign banking orgs.
- **NYDFS** — NY state financial regulator; aggressive on AML/sanctions.
- **AML** — Anti-Money Laundering controls.
- **BSA** — Bank Secrecy Act; core US AML law (records + reports). "BSA/AML."
- **OFAC** — sanctions enforcement; banks screen against sanctioned-party lists.
- **SAR** — Suspicious Activity Report (BOC's 2018 penalty = partly *late SARs*).
- **KYC** — Know Your Customer.
- **KRI — Key Risk Indicator** — metric that gives **early warning risk is rising** (overdue policy reviews, incomplete I-9s, training-completion). Different from KPI (performance).
- **ABAC** — **Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption** (gifts, conflicts, improper payments). *You'd coordinate HR's ABAC controls.*
- **Control** — a rule/process to prevent or detect a risk.
- **Control testing** — **check the control works: actual-vs-required, document gaps, corrective action, re-test.** ← *core of your job.*
- **Risk assessment** — identify what could go wrong, likelihood, impact, mitigating controls.
- **Corrective action** — the fix when a gap is found.
- **Continuous monitoring** — ongoing (not one-time) checking controls/vendors stay compliant.
- **TPRM (Third-Party Risk Mgmt)** — vendor lifecycle as risk: plan → due diligence → contract → **ongoing monitoring** → terminate. ← *your 10+ vendors map here.*
- **Record retention schedule** — documented how-long-kept + disposal policy (you'd maintain HR's).
- **Nepotism controls** — prevent conflicts from hiring/managing relatives (JD names "Head Office nepotism requirements").
- **Audit / regulatory exam** — Internal Audit (3rd line) or regulator (OCC/Fed/NYDFS) reviews; HR produces clean **deliverables/responses** (you'd support).
- **Recovery & Resolution Plan ("living will")** · **Data Governance Committee** · **Front Line Unit (FLU) Risk Manager** — recognize these; JD names them.
