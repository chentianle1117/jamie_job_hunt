## Rule 3 — Cover all locations every run
Remote US + Portland/Beaverton/Hillsboro + Seattle + relocation. Do NOT default to Portland-only or

## Rule 4 — Verify-before-trust (postings churn within HOURS)
2026-06-05 proof: 4 of 5 "found" no-account roles were DEAD by evening (404). ALWAYS re-verify a role
is live (Patchright fetch or authenticated browser) before tailoring/applying. A discovery list is a
lead list, not a confirmed list. Web-search-only sweeps under-perform — they can't see behind
LinkedIn/Greenhouse/PeopleAdmin logins; use the authenticated browser for the real inventory.

## Rule 5 — Route by APPLY-MECHANISM, not just fit
Tag every surfaced role:
- 🟢 **no-account** (Greenhouse/Lever/Ashby/BambooHR direct) → autopilot-friendly (captcha permitting)
- 🟡 **account-required** (Workday/iCIMS/SuccessFactors/CSOD/PeopleAdmin/custom) → goes to the
  account-creation queue (`ACCOUNT_QUEUE.md`); Claude CANNOT create accounts/passwords/solve CAPTCHA.
The account wall is the real throughput limit — surface it explicitly so the human can batch account
setup. Most high-fit roles (consulting + Nike/Adidas) are 🟡 by design.

## Rule 6 — Honesty over volume (the two enforced lessons)
- Rejections teach the filter: **Ramp** (Senior People & Culture Manager) → NO Senior/Manager titles
  outside the consulting-exception. **Persona** (Incentive Comp Analyst) → NO comp/total-rewards/
  payroll/benefits-admin primary functions. These are hard rejects now.
- Better to return 8 honest fits than 20 padded with senior/contract/comp/remote-saturated roles.
- Use the MCP (Gmail) each run to read application OUTCOMES (rejections/interviews) and feed the
  lesson back — that's the learning loop, not just discovery.
