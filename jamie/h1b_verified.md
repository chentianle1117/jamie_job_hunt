## 📋 Verification Methods

1. **myvisajobs.com** — Search company name, check LCA filings for HR/People/OD job categories
2. **JD language** — Look for "visa sponsorship available", "H1B", "immigration support"
3. **JD anti-language** — "Must be authorized to work in the US", "no visa sponsorship", "US citizens only" = ❌
4. **immi-canada type sites** — Cross-reference known sponsor lists
5. **Cap-exempt check** — Is the org a university, hospital system, nonprofit 501(c)(3), or research institution?
