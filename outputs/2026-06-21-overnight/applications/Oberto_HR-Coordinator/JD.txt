Oberto Snacks Inc. — Human Resources Coordinator
Location: Kent, WA (On-site, day plant)
Schedule: Full-time, day shift, 8:00 a.m.–4:30 p.m.
Pay: $25.00–$28.00 / hour
Source: LinkedIn (recommended) job id 4368843891; Yulys aggregator; oberto.com/careers (ADP Workforce Now)

PRIMARY FUNCTION / SUMMARY:
The HR Coordinator is an essential member of the HR team, providing exceptional service to employees
at all levels of the organization while ensuring accurate, timely, and seamless HR services. The
position is based out of the Kent plant and is responsible for providing general HR administrative
support, including ensuring the accuracy of information in the Human Resources Information System,
coordination and facilitation of new hire orientation, maintenance of employee files, and
coordination of employee communication and HR-related programs and events.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES / RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Maintain the Human Resource Information System (ADP) with accurate information including new hires,
  status changes, rate changes, terminations, etc.
- Assist with coordination and facilitation of new hire orientations.
- Maintain all employee personnel files.
- Provide general employee and management support through answering questions pertaining to HR-related topics.
- Plan and create content for a variety of internal communications mediums, such as the company intranet,
  bulletin boards, toolbox talks, newsletters, or email.
- Assist with employee recognition programs.
- Maintain the HR intranet site.
- Act as the Commuter Trip Reduction Program Coordinator.
- Assist with HR projects and other duties as assigned.

QUALIFICATIONS / REQUIREMENTS:
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources or a related field preferred, or equivalent experience.
- 1–2 years of experience with administrative Human Resources duties preferred.
- Knowledge of HRIS systems and HR processes, and of applicable state and federal employment laws.
- Microsoft Office computer software applications at a medium to expert user level.
- Excellent organizational skills required; strong attention to detail and customer-service focus;
  ability to manage confidential information while meeting deadlines.

BENEFITS:
Medical, dental, vision, prescription coverage; short- and long-term disability; life insurance;
wellness program; Flex spending account; Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) with contribution match;
401(k) match; PTO; tuition reimbursement; fitness center access; employee discounts.
