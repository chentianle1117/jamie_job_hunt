OVERVIEW
The Learning & Development (L&D) Specialist is responsible for building role-based training
curriculums, managing L&D workflows, and developing high-quality content. Both strategic and
hands-on — drives learning initiatives for both employees and customers in a high-growth environment.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Plan, prioritize, and manage the full inventory of internal and external training courses.
- Collaborate with HR, Product, Delivery, and Customer Support to align content creation with
  business objectives and audience needs.
- Maintain the Learning Inventory as content is prioritized, planned, and launched.
- Design and maintain training courses, certificate programs, and learning resources in accordance
  with best practices (e.g., ADDIE model).
- Partner with subject-matter experts to identify training needs and translate technical knowledge
  into digestible learning modules.
- Support the delivery of diverse training formats, including Instructor-Led (ILT), Virtual (VILT),
  and Train-the-Trainer programs.
- Build and execute project plans to ensure L&D objectives are delivered on schedule and within spec.
- Oversee and manage learning tools, specifically the eLeap and EasyLlama LMS platforms and
  associated authoring tools.
- Measure the effectiveness of learning programs and optimize content based on data-driven insights.

REQUIREMENTS & QUALIFICATIONS
- Minimum of 5 years of related experience in learning strategy, instructional design, and project
  management in a corporate environment.
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Psychology, Education, Business, or equivalent professional
  experience.
- Demonstrated ability to translate organizational objectives into training for a diverse,
  cross-functional audience.
- Proactive problem-solver who can adapt to a high-growth environment and translate complex business
  needs into engaging learning experiences.
