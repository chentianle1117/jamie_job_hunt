=== JOB DESCRIPTION ===

Nielsen seeks a Change Manager to support organizational transformation by helping staff adopt new tools and processes. The role emphasizes executing change strategies through practical communications, stakeholder engagement, training coordination, and adoption initiatives across the company.

=== KEY RESPONSIBILITIES ===

- Execute change management plans for technology and process initiatives
- Identify impacted stakeholders and support readiness activities
- Develop stakeholder lists, communication plans, and adoption metrics
- Create audience-appropriate communications like announcements and FAQs
- Coordinate training activities with subject matter experts
- Assess change impacts and document team effects
- Monitor feedback, identify resistance patterns, escalate risks
- Support engagement sessions and training workshops
- Measure adoption indicators including participation and feedback trends
- Build Nielsen process knowledge and strengthen change management skills

=== REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS ===

- 2-5 years experience in tech project management, change management, training, or technical transformation
- Advanced proficiency in Google Workspace tools
- Experience supporting technology implementations or process improvements
- Strong written and verbal communication abilities
- Organizational skills managing multiple deliverables
- Analytical mindset for identifying trends and summarizing feedback
- Familiarity with change management principles and stakeholder engagement
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience
