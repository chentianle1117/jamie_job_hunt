COMPANY INTRO
================================================================================
At A-dec, we do more than create the highest quality products and services for the dental industry; we strive to deliver a superior employment experience for each of our team members. With an environment that encourages and assists each person in developing to their highest potential, a career at A-dec is incredibly rewarding.

================================================================================
JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================
- Partner with leaders and internal customers to assess business, performance, and development needs, using data, assessments, observation, and stakeholder input to recommend appropriate learning and organizational development solutions.
- Design, develop, and deliver learning and development solutions using a variety of methods, including classroom, virtual, blended, and on-the-job learning, grounded in adult learning principles and aligned with business needs.
- Support organizational and team change efforts by applying practical change management approaches, including stakeholder engagement, communication planning, leader enablement, and reinforcement strategies.
- Facilitate workshops, training sessions, and working sessions focused on leadership development, team effectiveness, onboarding, change initiatives, and skill development.
- Collaborate with People & Culture Operations, Corporate Communications, and HR Business Partner colleagues to ensure alignment of learning, change, and people practices.
- Lead and manage learning and development projects, including project planning, coordination, communication, execution, and evaluation of outcomes and effectiveness.
- Measure and evaluate the effectiveness of learning and development solutions; gather feedback, document lessons learned and apply continuous improvement practices.
- Support leaders and teams through coaching, facilitation, and consultation related to learning, development, and applied OD practices.
- Maintain current knowledge of learning, organizational development, and change management best practices, tools, and technologies, and apply them appropriately to A-dec's context.
- Build and maintain strong, collaborative relationships with stakeholders, bringing teams to alignment and action while maintaining a people-centered, values-aligned approach.

================================================================================
QUALIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
- Working knowledge of applied organizational development and change management practices.
- Strong project management skills with the ability to manage multiple initiatives simultaneously.
- Excellent interpersonal, facilitation, verbal, and written communication skills.
- Ability to build trust and credibility with leaders and partners across the organization.
- Ability to work autonomously while knowing when to escalate or seek guidance.
- Proficiency with authoring, graphics, sound and video editing software to create, maintain, and enhance online training, preferred.
- Bilingual in Spanish and English preferred.
- Familiarity with learning management systems, blended learning design, and instructional design methodologies, preferred.

Education and Experience:
- Generally requires a Bachelor's degree with 5+ years of experience in learning and development, organizational development, training, or a related field. Preference given to those with a Bachelor's degree or Master's degree in Organizational Development, Instructional Design, Human Resources, or related field.
- Demonstrated experience designing and delivering learning programs and facilitating groups.
- Experience supporting organizational or enterprise-wide change initiatives.
- Experience using assessments or tools to support leadership or team development, preferred.
