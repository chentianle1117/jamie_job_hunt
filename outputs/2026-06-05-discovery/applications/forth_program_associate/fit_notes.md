## Sponsorship Caveat

**HIGH RISK.** Forth is a small Portland nonprofit (~$3–5M budget range typical for orgs this size). Small nonprofits almost never sponsor H-1B visas — the USCIS filing fees alone (~$5–7K+) are prohibitive for a $50K role. The JD does not mention E-Verify or sponsorship explicitly (neither a confirmation nor a denial).

**Recommendation:** Jamie should contact Forth directly before or immediately after applying to ask: "I want to confirm this role is open to candidates who require H-1B sponsorship." Do not submit blindly and spend goodwill if they cannot sponsor. This is the single biggest risk to the application.
