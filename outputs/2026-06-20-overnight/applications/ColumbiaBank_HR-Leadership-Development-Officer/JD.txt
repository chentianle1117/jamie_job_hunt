About the job

About The Role

The Leadership Development Partner designs and delivers programs that build leadership capability across the organization. This role facilitates impactful learning experiences (virtual and in-person), partners with stakeholders to identify development needs, and implements solutions that strengthen current and future leaders.


Design and deliver engaging leadership programs across in-person, virtual, and e-learning formats using adult learning principles
Develop and execute leadership programs aligned with organizational goals and leadership frameworks
Partner with stakeholders to assess needs and address leadership skill gaps
Measure program effectiveness and continuously improve content using data, feedback, and evaluation tools
Coach and support leaders and internal facilitators to strengthen program delivery
Manage end-to-end program operations (enrollment, communications, materials, facilitation, and reporting)
Utilize LMS to track participation, assess needs, and report outcomes
Maintain vendor relationships and manage program budgets effectively
Oversee course logistics (rosters, attendance, materials, scheduling)
Monitor enrollment trends and drive engagement through targeted outreach
Stay current on leadership development trends and apply best practices
Support administrative tasks with a focus on accuracy and efficiency


About You


Bachelor’s degree in HR, Organizational Development, Business, or related field (or equivalent experience)
Master’s degree preferred
5+ years of strong facilitation and presentation skills with the ability to engage diverse audiences
Experience designing and delivering leadership development programs
Knowledge of leadership frameworks and adult learning principles
Proficiency with LMS platforms and training tools
Data-driven mindset with the ability to measure program effectiveness
Strong communication, collaboration, and project management skills
Ability to adapt in a fast-paced, evolving environment
Certification in coaching or learning & development (e.g., ICF, ATD, CCL, CPTD)
Travel will be required for training sessions, workshops, and meetings
