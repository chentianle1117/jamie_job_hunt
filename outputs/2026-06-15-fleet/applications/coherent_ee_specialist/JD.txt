ROLE SUMMARY
The Employee Experience Specialist designs and runs the programs that shape how employees experience Coherent across the full lifecycle. This is a corporate HR / People role (no ITAR or export-control requirement). The role partners with HR and business leaders to build engagement, recognition, wellness, and culture programs grounded in employee data.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design, launch, and run employee engagement, recognition, and wellness programs.
- Own employee lifecycle programs including onboarding and offboarding.
- Lead DEIB (Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Belonging) and culture initiatives.
- Develop and execute internal communications that reinforce culture and engagement.
- Analyze employee survey and pulse engagement data and translate findings into action.
- Partner cross-functionally with HR business partners and business leaders to deliver people programs.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Communications, Psychology, Organizational Development, or a related field.
- 3–5 years of experience in employee experience, HR, people operations, engagement, or internal communications.
- Experience designing and running people/culture programs and analyzing engagement data.
- Strong cross-functional collaboration and communication skills.
