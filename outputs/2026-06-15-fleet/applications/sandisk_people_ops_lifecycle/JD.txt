People Operations & Employee Lifecycle Specialist — Sandisk

COMPANY DESCRIPTION
Sandisk innovates in Flash and advanced memory technologies, serving as "the beating heart of the digital world." The company operates two World Economic Forum-recognized Global Lighthouse Network facilities and maintains global manufacturing and supply chain capabilities.

JOB DESCRIPTION — ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

HR Operations — Employee Lifecycle Services
- Design, standardize, and improve global operational processes across hiring, onboarding, employee changes, and offboarding
- Ensure disciplined termination operations including manager enablement, documentation, and regulatory compliance
- Drive continuous improvement using Lean/Six Sigma methodologies
- Identify automation and AI-enabled capability opportunities
- Standardize documentation and training materials
- Implement operational metrics, dashboards, and performance indicators
- Maintain audit-ready processes and documentation standards

Policy Operationalization & Compliance
- Operationalize people policies through workflows and controls
- Draft and revise HR policies ensuring global consistency
- Support communication and change-management efforts

QUALIFICATIONS

Required:
- Bachelor's degree in HR, IT, Business Administration, or equivalent
- 5+ years of Workday and ServiceNow experience with business process and functional expertise
- Strong project management capabilities with mid-sized systems projects
- Exceptional communication and stakeholder management skills
- Ability to manage ambiguity and competing priorities
- Strategic thinking and problem-solving abilities
- Experience in HR Operations or People Operations
- Formal process mapping and documentation experience
- Lean, Kaizen, Six Sigma, or operational excellence experience
