ROLE SUMMARY
The Faculty Onboarding Specialist in the BSD Office of Faculty and Physician Recruiting and
Onboarding (OFPRO) coordinates the onboarding lifecycle for newly-hired faculty and physicians,
from offer acceptance through the first 90 days. This individual serves as the primary
onboarding point of contact for incoming faculty.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Coordinate the end-to-end onboarding lifecycle for newly-hired faculty and physicians, from
  offer acceptance through the first 90 days.
- Serve as the primary onboarding contact for incoming faculty, providing a welcoming,
  organized, and responsive experience.
- Track documentation and training completion against deadlines; ensure all required steps are
  completed.
- Coordinate across HR, IT, credentialing, payroll, and benefits to complete onboarding
  requirements.
- Set up systems access, workspace, ID, and badge for new hires.
- Maintain accurate onboarding records.
- Assist with faculty orientation communications.

MINIMUM / REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Minimum 2-5 years of experience (preferred 2-4 years) in HR operations, onboarding, and/or
  academic administration.
- Bachelor's degree preferred.
- Strong organizational, coordination, and communication skills; ability to track many moving
  parts and deadlines.
- Comfort with HRIS / onboarding systems and maintaining records.

APPLICATION
Resume required; cover letter preferred.
