People Operations Coordinator — Tekmetric
Remote or Houston, Texas

POSITION OVERVIEW
Remote or Houston, Texas position supporting the People & Culture team across recruiting, onboarding, and employee experience.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Recruiting Coordination (~20 hrs/week)
- Manage complex interview scheduling across multiple time zones in various formats
- Coordinate onsite interview logistics including rooms, access, travel, and accommodations
- Serve as primary contact for candidates and interviewers during scheduling changes
- Ensure consistent, high-quality candidate experience throughout interview process
- Implement AI and automation improvements to scheduling processes

New Hire Onboarding (~8-10 hrs/week)
- Execute end-to-end onboarding logistics including travel, accommodations, and training sessions
- Manage I-9 completion and verification; maintain accurate HRIS records
- Track mandatory training completion to 100%

Culture & Engagement (~4-5 hrs/week)
- Administer employee recognition programs and pulse surveys
- Process swag, postal, and gift card requests

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
- 2-4 years in recruiting coordination, HR/people operations, or comparable roles
- Experience scheduling high-volume, multi-interviewer interviews across time zones
- Strong organizational and triaging skills
- HRIS/HCM working knowledge preferred
- Exceptional attention to detail
- Professional written and verbal communication
- Interest in leveraging AI tools for productivity

SCREENING QUESTIONS

- HRIS/HCM platform experience?
- I-9 verification or employee records experience?
- Salary expectations?
- Work authorization status?
- Houston location or hybrid capability?
