Our Mission:

         We Connect Neighbors, Building Community and Improve Lives

         Neighborhood House hires truly amazing people. 

Every day, we work to end poverty, inequity and social isolation. We create connections and help people from diverse backgrounds access the resources they need to build more self-determined lives.

 We know that our work is essential! We see children learning and thriving in our education and child care programs, people benefitting from healthy food and stable housing, and older adults living more independently. That’s why we are motivated to go above and beyond every day.

 Neighborhood House is an equal opportunity employer.  We strongly encourage people from all backgrounds and stages of life to consider working with us.
