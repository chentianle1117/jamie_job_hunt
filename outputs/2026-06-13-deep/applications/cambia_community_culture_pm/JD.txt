Who We Are Looking For

Every day, Cambia's team is living our mission to make health care easier and lives better. As part of the Human Resources team, our Culture & Community Program Manager plays a hands-on role in bringing that mission to life in the everyday experiences of our employees. This role sits at the intersection of culture, inclusion, employee connection, and community. At Cambia, inclusion isn't the responsibility of a single role — it's an organizational commitment. This position helps translate Cambia’s commitment to inclusion and connection into employee programs, experiences, and practices across the organization. We’re looking for someone who believes belonging isn't a program — it's how we work.

If you're energized by building community, connecting people to purpose, and making the workplace feel like somewhere worth showing up, this role is for you

Qualifications

What You Bring to Cambia


3–5 years of experience in employee engagement, culture, inclusion, talent, communications, or related HR/people functions, with experience embedding inclusive practices into employee or talent programs
Experience managing programs or events with multiple stakeholders and moving parts
Familiarity with Employee Resource Groups, employee communities, or volunteer/foundation partnerships a plus
Strong organizational and project management skills, with the ability to manage multiple priorities and coordinate employee programs with consistency and care


Skills And Attributes


Ability to translate culture and inclusion priorities into practical people practices and employee experiences across the talent lifecycle
Strong understanding of how inclusive practices show up in hiring, onboarding, development, engagement and leadership behavior
Strong relationship-building skills, with the ability to work effectively across teams and foster inclusive employee experiences
Ability to build credibility and work effectively across levels, from frontline employees to senior leaders
Strong execution skills, with attention to detail and the ability to connect day-to-day work to broader culture and business priorities
Demonstrated commitment to inclusive workplace practices and employee engagement
Strong communicator — written, verbal, and in a room full of people


What You Will Do

ERG & Employee Community Support


Serve as the primary HR point of contact and program manager for Cambia's Employee Resource Groups
Support ERG leaders with programming, logistics, development resources, and visibility
Help grow and sustain employee clubs and communities across the organization


Culture Integration & Belonging


Lead program management for culture integration initiatives that connect employees across teams, locations, and backgrounds
Support return-to-office efforts by creating meaningful in-office experiences that foster connection — not just presence
Drive expansion of Blue Squad and similar community-building programs to all Cambia office locations


Events & Experiences


Manage planning and execution of employee engagement events,
Partner with internal teams to design experiences that reflect and reinforce Cambia's culture and values


Community Impact


Partner with the Cambia Foundation to identify and build community-based cultural opportunities that extend our values beyond our walls
Connect employee volunteerism and community engagement to the broader culture strategy


Cross-Functional Collaboration


Partner with Talent, HRBPs, and Communications to integrate culture and inclusion into key talent practices and employee experience moments
Help embed belonging and inclusive leadership principles into how Cambia hires, develops, recognizes, and supports employees
Work closely with HR Business Partners, Communications, and senior leaders to align culture efforts with business priorities
Serve as a resource and thought partner to leaders who want to strengthen culture within their teams
