With a master's in Applied Organizational Psychology, I approach employee experience as a system to study: diagnose where people get stuck, design the moment that fixes it, then measure whether it landed. I rebuilt it around how people actually worked and cut onboarding time **75%**. That's the lesson I'd bring to Day 1 here—you don't design the experience you imagine, you design the one your customer (the new hire) actually needs, and you test it early instead of waiting for it to be perfect.
